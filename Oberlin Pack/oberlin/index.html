Where compassionate care meets unparalleled expertise.

Palliative care is a specialized form of medical care that aims to provide relief from the symptoms and stress of serious illnesses. The goal is to improve quality of life for both the patient and the family. It is appropriate at any age and at any stage in a serious illness and can be provided along with curative treatment.


Our team at "Palliative Care from the Heart" is made up of specialized medical professionals who work with the patient's other providers to provide an extra layer of support. We focus on providing relief from the symptoms, pain, and emotional stress that can come with a serious illness. This includes managing shortness of breath, fatigue, anxiety, depression, nausea, insomnia, and loss of appetite.


In addition, we provide emotional and psychological support, helping patients and their families to navigate the healthcare system, make tough medical decisions, and manage the stress that can come with a serious illness. Our aim is to ensure that patients and families feel seen, heard, and cared for.

Long Term Care Facilities

At Palliative Care from the Heart, we understand the unique needs of individuals in long-term care facilities. That's why we offer specialized palliative care services specifically tailored for these settings. Our dedicated team collaborates closely with the existing staff at long-term care facilities to ensure a seamless integration of our services. We focus on providing comfort, attention, and personalized care planning to enhance the quality of life for residents in these facilities. If you're seeking a dedicated palliative care team experienced in working within long-term care facilities, Palliative Care from the Heart is here to meet your needs.

Telehealth Services

Our telehealth palliative care services bring our expertise directly to you, wherever you may be. At Palliative Care from the Heart, we understand the challenges that come with traveling for appointments, especially for those with serious illnesses. That's why we offer virtual consultations and support through our telehealth services. Our telehealth capabilities allow us to respond on short notice when care is urgently needed. With telehealth, we provide the same high-quality palliative care you would receive in person, but through a secure, virtual platform. Whether you are in a long-term care facility or your own home, our telehealth palliative care services offer a convenient and accessible option for receiving the care you need. If you're seeking telehealth palliative care services that prioritize your comfort and care, reach out to Palliative Care from the Heart today.

Home Visits

Experience compassionate and expert palliative care in the comfort of your own home with our home visit services. Our team at Palliative Care from the Heart understands the importance of providing high-quality care in familiar surroundings. Our home visit palliative care service allows patients to remain in their own homes while receiving the highest standard of care. Our experienced specialists travel to homes to provide these services, reducing stress and disruption for both patients and their loved ones. If you're seeking a palliative care provider offering home visits, reach out to Palliative Care from the Heart today.

Jennifer Zemola, the driving force behind Palliative Care from the Heart, brings a wealth of experience and a deep passion for delivering compassionate care to those facing serious illness. With a strong background in critical care, Jennifer began her career as an ICU nurse, dedicating many years to providing comfort and support to patients and families during their most vulnerable moments.


Recognizing the profound impact that palliative care can have on the lives of patients and families, Jennifer transitioned her focus to this specialized field. Her years of expertise in delivering outstanding palliative care services have earned her a reputation for excellence, compassion, and a commitment to going above and beyond for her patients.


As the CEO of Palliative Care from the Heart, Jennifer leads a multidisciplinary team of professionals, ensuring that the core values of empathy, dignity, and personalized care are at the heart of every interaction. Her dedication to providing premier palliative care services is evident in the lasting relationships she builds with patients, families, and her team."
